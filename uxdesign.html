poot: collaborative video conferencing (CMPT 363)
problem statement
among the widely used video conferencing applications, none are particularly geared towards remote learning. for this project, our group aimed to remedy this concern: our goal was to facilitate a space within our novel video conferencing application that allowed our users to work on assignments together, without ever needing to leave the application.
user research
since the aim of our project was to design a tool to promote collaboration in an online educational environment, we conducted a survey which targeted university students and instructors. we created the survey using SurveyMonkey, and distributed the link online. after 20 completed surveys, we compiled the results and used them to determine user requirements.
